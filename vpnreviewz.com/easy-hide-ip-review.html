Easy-Hide-IP Review

Easy-Hide-IP offers a VPN service that provides you with anonymity online. This is a full VPN service, so all of your traffic is encrypted and you don’t have to worry about prying eyes from your ISP or from anyone else on the Internet, for that matter.

Easy-Hide-IP vs. The Competition

Easy-Hide-IP takes Liberty Reserve payments, which makes them a good option for those who don’t want to use PayPal or a credit card to buy their VPN service. Their pricing is competitive, to be sure, and they offer unlimited bandwidth, which are all positives.

Easy-Hide-IP Conclusion

Easy-Hide-IP is a good service and it may work for you if you’re on the Liberty Reserve payment system. It also may work for you if you want something simple and easy to use and want to purchase ahead of time so you don’t have to keep buying VPN service every month.

The service has good encryption and is easy enough to use for inexperienced VPN users, so it does have its benefits.
